Review and select the STEM project scenarios you would implement

You will explore ten STEM project scenarios enhanced with GenAI. Your role is to review them and select the ones that best fit your classroom context, your students’ needs, and your teaching interests. There are no right answers. We are interested in your professional judgement.

What you will do

Follow a simple process to review the project scenarios and submit your choices.

1

Explore

Open the project cards and review the core idea, learning flow, and student output.

2

Reflect

Think about your students, subject area, classroom realities, and local context.

3

Evaluate

Complete the evaluation form and select the four project scenarios you would be most interested in implementing.

4

Shape the APK

Your choices will help shape the final set of projects developed for implementation.
